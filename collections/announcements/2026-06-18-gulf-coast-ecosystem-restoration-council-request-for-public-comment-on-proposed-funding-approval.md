The Gulf Coast Ecosystem Restoration Council (Council) is seeking public comment on a proposal to approve additional funding for the ongoing Pensacola Bay Living Shoreline Phase 1 implementation project in Escambia County, Florida. Specifically, the Council is proposing to approve $748,010 in additional funding to cover construction, engineering, and inspection costs associated with sand excavation and signage requirements for this living shoreline project.
