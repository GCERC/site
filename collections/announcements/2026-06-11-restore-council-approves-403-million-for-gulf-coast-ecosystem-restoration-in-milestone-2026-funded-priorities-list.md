---
title: RESTORE Council Approves $403 Million for Gulf Coast Ecosystem
  Restoration in Milestone 2026 Funded Priorities List
date: 2026-06-11
url: https://content.govdelivery.com/accounts/USRESTORECOUNCIL/bulletins/41b8dc0
---
On June 10, 2026, the Gulf Coast Ecosystem Restoration Council (RESTORE Council) voted unanimously to approve the 2026 Funded Priorities List (FPL), committing $403,654,000 in new funding for 19 ecosystem restoration projects and programs across the Gulf Coast. With this vote, the Council has now committed over $1 Billion for restoration activities under the Council-Selected Restoration Component of the RESTORE Act.
