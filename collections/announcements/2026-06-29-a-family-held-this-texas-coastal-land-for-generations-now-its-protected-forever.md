Original Article by [Ariana Garcia](https://www.chron.com/author/ariana-garcia/?utm_medium=email&utm_source=govdelivery), Assistant News Editor for CHRON. [Original article](https://www.chron.com/wildlife/article/justin-hurst-wildlife-management-area-expansion-22315294.php?utm_medium=email&utm_source=govdelivery) posted on June 22, 2026

[One of the Texas coast's largest](https://www.chron.com/wildlife/article/galveston-manta-ray-why-22291292.php?utm_medium=email&utm_source=govdelivery) remaining expanses of prairie, wetlands and tidal marsh just got bigger. A 2,500-acre [conservation deal](https://www.chron.com/gulf-coast/article/texas-gulf-coast-recreational-area-22311913.php?utm_medium=email&utm_source=govdelivery) has expanded the [Justin Hurst Wildlife Management Area](https://tpwd.texas.gov/huntwild/hunt/wma/find_a_wma/list/?id=41&utm_medium=email&utm_source=govdelivery) near Freeport to more than 17,500 acres.
