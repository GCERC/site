---
title: RESTORE Funding Approved for Water Quality Project on University of South
  Alabama Campus
date: 2026-04-27
url: https://content.govdelivery.com/accounts/USRESTORECOUNCIL/bulletins/4147a69
---
On April 22, 2026, the Gulf Coast Ecosystem Restoration Council voted to approve funding for a water quality project on the University of South Alabama campus in Mobile, Alabama. Specifically, the Council approved $349,133 to install stormwater management systems, referred to as bioswales, at the USA campus.
