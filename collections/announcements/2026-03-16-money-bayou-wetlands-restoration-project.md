The U.S. Department of Commerce (DOC), through the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) Restoration Center, has been awarded $1.24 million RESTORE Act Council-Selected Restoration Component funds to support the Money Bayou Wetlands Restoration Project. This project is a key part of NOAA's *Connecting Coastal Waters (CCW)* initiative, which partners with local organizations to restore the extent, functionality, and resilience of Gulf Coast wetlands.
